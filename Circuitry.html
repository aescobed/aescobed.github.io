I designed and created a working electromagnetic motor with my peers by applying Faraday's law. I incorporated a 3D printed rotor with attached magnets along with an arduino connected to turned wire to impart an electromagnetic force onto the magnets, therefore spinning the rotor. We challenged ourselves not to use any sensors which is why the motor is turning slowly since it is just timed.
